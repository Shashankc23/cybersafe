👁️ Privacy

Guard Your Personal Info!

Learn three simple ways to protect your personal information and stay safe online.

📍 Thing 1: Keep Personal Details Private

Information like your school, home address, phone number, and town can help strangers figure out where you are in real life.

Even if someone seems friendly online, you only know what they've chosen to tell you.

💡 Remember
Never share your address, school, phone number, or daily location with people you meet online.

📸 Thing 2: Photos Can Reveal More Than You Think

Photos taken on phones and tablets can sometimes include hidden location information called GPS data.

If that information is shared online, it may reveal exactly where the photo was taken.

💡 Remember
Ask a parent or trusted adult before posting photos online, and keep location services turned off when sharing pictures.

📋 Thing 3: Be Careful with Online Forms

Fake competitions and giveaways often ask for your full name, address, phone number, or email to collect personal information.

A prize isn't worth giving away your privacy.

⚠️ Watch Out
Never fill out a form asking for personal information without asking a trusted adult first.
