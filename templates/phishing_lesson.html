🪝 Phishing

Don't Get Hooked!

Learn the three most common tricks scammers use before testing your skills.

🎭 Trick 1: Pretending to Be Someone Else

Phishing is when a scammer pretends to be a real company or person — like Roblox, YouTube, or even your school — to trick you into giving away information.

They copy logos, colours, and names to look real. But they are not.

💡 Remember
Always ask yourself: "Did I expect this message?"

🔗 Trick 2: Fake Website Addresses

Scammers use website addresses that look almost right, but have a sneaky difference.

  • Real: roblox.com   |   Fake: r0blox.com (zero instead of the letter o!)
  • Real: minecraft.net   |   Fake: minecraft-helpdesk.net
💡 Remember
Always check the address bar before clicking a link or entering a password.

⏰ Trick 3: Fake Urgency

Scammers try to rush you so you don't have time to think. Watch out for messages like:

  • "Your account will be DELETED in 1 hour!"
  • "Claim your prize RIGHT NOW before it expires!"
  • "Act immediately or lose access forever!"
⚠️ Watch Out
If a message makes you panic or rush, slow down and tell a trusted adult.
