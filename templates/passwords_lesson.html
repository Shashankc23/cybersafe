🔐 Passwords

Your Secret Key!

Learn the three habits that make passwords strong and keep your accounts safe.

💀 Thing 1: Common Passwords Get Cracked in Seconds

Hackers try the world's most common passwords first. Passwords like password123 and 123456 can be guessed almost instantly.

If millions of people use the same password, it isn't really a secret.

💡 Remember
Never use simple passwords like password123, 123456, or easy number patterns.

🎮 Thing 2: Personal Details Are Easy to Guess

Your favorite game, pet's name, birthday, or school may seem like a good password—but they're often easy for someone else to guess.

Even passwords like minecraft2014 reveal information about you.

💡 Remember
Avoid using your name, birthday, favorite game, pet's name, or anything someone could learn about you.

💪 Thing 3: Strong Passwords Use Four Ingredients

Great passwords combine different types of characters to make them much harder to guess.

  • Uppercase letters (A–Z)
  • Lowercase letters (a–z)
  • Numbers (0–9)
  • Symbols (! @ # $ %)
💡 Remember
A password like MyD0g$N@medMax! is much stronger than max123 because it mixes letters, numbers, and symbols while still being easy to remember.
